Josh Morin's Cooking Book

As a collection of some of my best recipes, I made this cookbook for the beginner cook who wishes to broaden his/her cooking abiliies. I picture those who are cooking for a loved one for the first time and want to make it special, or maybe just someone cooking out of love for food. This online cookbook allows those in need of some savory, easy to prepare meals to create just that from scratch.
